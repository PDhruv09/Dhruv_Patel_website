First Project:

Title

Does Urbanization effect Power Outage?

Discription

"Does Urbanization Affect Power Outages?" is an analytical project designed for the DSC 80 course at UCSD. The study examines major power outages in the United States from January 2000 to July 2016, utilizing data from Purdue University's Laboratory for Advancing Sustainable Critical Infrastructure.By investigating the correlation between urbanization and power outages, this project aims to uncover patterns and provide insights that can help improve infrastructure planning and disaster preparedness. The findings are intended to benefit policymakers, utility companies, and the general public in their efforts to enhance the resilience of electrical grids.
